# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a harassment-free, respectful, and constructive project for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and technically honest.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Welcome questions and acknowledge uncertainty.
- Protect private information and disclose security issues privately.
- Accept maintainer decisions made to preserve project safety and scope.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, or publishing another person's private information is not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through GitHub's private reporting/contact mechanisms. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This code applies in project spaces and when someone officially represents the project elsewhere.
